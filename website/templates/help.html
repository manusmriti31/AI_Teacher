Frequently Asked Questions

How do I create a new notebook?

Click on the "Create New Notebook" button from your dashboard or notebooks section. Choose a subject and customize your learning path.

How is my progress tracked?

Your progress is automatically tracked as you complete lessons, quizzes, and exercises. View detailed statistics in your profile.

Can I study offline?

Yes, you can download lessons for offline access. Your progress will sync when you reconnect to the internet.
